5 Ways’: Street Theatre performance completed for a commission by the Faculty of Music and Fine Arts, at The University of Melbourne. It was exhibited as part of the First Commissions showcase at the Martin Myer Arena and for the Keith &amp; Elisabeth Murdoch Travelling Fellowship exhibition at The Margaret Lawrence Gallery. As seen in two parts on the firstcommisions.com.au[https://firstcommissions.com.au/titanic/carmen-sibha-keiso#response] online gallery, where the video-document and action-transcript are available.
